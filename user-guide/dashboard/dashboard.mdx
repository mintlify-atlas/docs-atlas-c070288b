The OwnPay Admin Dashboard is your command center for the entire payment gateway operation. Every time you log in, this is your landing page — a real-time snapshot of transaction volumes, revenue figures, customer counts, and pending activity across your platform. It supports both a global aggregated view spanning all brands and a focused per-brand view, allowing super-administrators and brand-scoped staff alike to get the financial picture they need without navigating deep into individual reports.

## Getting to the Dashboard

After a successful [login](/user-guide/auth/login), you land on the Dashboard automatically. If you are already in another section, click **Dashboard** at the top of the left sidebar to return. The dashboard URL follows your configured domain at the root or `/dashboard` path.

## Dashboard Layout Overview

The page is divided into three primary functional zones:

### 1. Header Navigation Bar

The bar running across the top of the page contains global controls accessible from anywhere in the platform:

| Control | Description |
|---|---|
| **Sidebar Toggle** | Hides or expands the left navigation sidebar to maximize screen real estate. |
| **Brand Context Dropdown** | Switches between **Global View** (all brands, super-admin only) and individual brand/store views. |
| **Global Search** | Full-text search across transactions, customers, and invoices. |
| **Theme Toggle** | Switches between Dark and Light mode for comfortable viewing in any environment. |
| **User Menu** | Access account settings or log out. |

### 2. Key Performance Indicator (KPI) Cards

Beneath the header, a row of metric cards shows aggregated statistics for the **selected brand** and **time range**:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Total Revenue" icon="circle-dollar-to-slot" href="/user-guide/payments/transactions">
    The cumulative value of all **completed** transactions converted to your base currency. This figure reflects real settled income — pending and failed transactions are excluded.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Completed Transactions" icon="circle-check" href="/user-guide/payments/transactions">
    The count of transactions that were successfully processed and confirmed by the payment gateway. Use this alongside total revenue to understand your average transaction value.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pending Transactions" icon="clock" href="/user-guide/payments/transactions">
    Transactions where the customer initiated checkout but payment has not yet been confirmed. A high pending count may indicate a gateway connectivity issue or an offline SMS parsing device.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Total Customers" icon="users" href="/user-guide/dashboard/dashboard">
    The number of unique customers registered under the selected brand context. Tracks customer acquisition over the chosen time period.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

A **Time Range Selector** above the KPI cards controls the period covered. Available options are **Today**, **Last 7 Days**, **Last 30 Days**, and **All Time**.

<Warning>
  Always confirm which brand is selected in the **Brand Context Dropdown** before reading the KPI cards. Viewing a single brand's metrics while the Global View is active — or vice versa — leads to reporting errors, especially when preparing financial summaries.
</Warning>

### 3. Recent Transactions List

Below the KPI cards, the dashboard displays the most recent payment attempts in a scannable table. This lets you quickly verify incoming payments without navigating to the full transactions page.

Each row in the recent transactions list shows:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **TRX ID** | The system-generated unique transaction identifier (e.g. `TXN-20260501-0042`). Click to open the full transaction detail view. |
| **Customer** | The customer name, email address, or phone number associated with the payment. |
| **Amount** | The transaction amount and currency (e.g. `1,500.00 BDT`). |
| **Gateway** | The payment processor used (e.g. bKash, Nagad, Stripe, or a manual gateway link). |
| **Status** | A color-coded badge: green for `completed`, yellow for `pending`, red for `cancelled` or `failed`. |
| **Date** | The exact creation timestamp of the transaction. |

A **View All** link at the bottom of the list navigates to the full [Transactions](/user-guide/payments/transactions) page.

## Filtering Dashboard Metrics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a brand context">
    Click the **Brand Selector** dropdown in the header navigation bar. Choose a specific brand (e.g. `My Custom Brand`) to scope all KPI cards and the recent transactions list to that brand's data. Select **Global View** to see aggregated metrics across all brands (super-admin only).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the time range">
    Find the **Time Range Selector** in the main content area (above the KPI cards). Click it and choose from **Today**, **Last 7 Days**, **Last 30 Days**, or **All Time**. The KPI cards refresh instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the KPI cards">
    Read the updated Total Revenue, Completed, Pending, and Customers figures. These reflect only the selected brand and time window.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Inspecting a Recent Transaction

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the transaction">
    Scroll to the **Recent Transactions** table and identify the row you want to review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the transaction row">
    Click anywhere on the row (or the **TRX ID** link) to open the transaction details panel. This shows customer metadata, gateway fee breakdowns, the net amount credited to your ledger, and the gateway's own transaction ID.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cross-reference with the Ledger">
    For financial reconciliation, click the **Ledger** link within the transaction detail panel or navigate to [Ledger](/user-guide/payments/ledger) and search by the transaction reference to see the corresponding double-entry journal postings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Global View vs. Brand Context

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Global View">
    **Available to:** Super-administrators only.

    The Global View aggregates financial data across **all registered brands** in your OwnPay instance. Use this when:
    - Comparing revenue performance between white-label store domains.
    - Auditing the total platform-wide transaction volume.
    - Generating executive-level financial reports.

    <Info>
      Staff accounts assigned to specific brands cannot access the Global View. The dropdown will only show the brands they are authorized for.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Brand Context">
    **Available to:** All authenticated users.

    Selecting a specific brand filters all repository queries to a single `merchant_id`. The KPI cards, recent transactions list, and search results all reflect only that brand's activity. This is the default mode for brand-scoped staff members.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Best Practices

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Reconcile with Ledger Weekly" icon="scale-balanced" href="/user-guide/payments/ledger">
    Cross-check the Total Revenue figure on the dashboard against the current balance on the Ledger page. Discrepancies may indicate unposted manual gateway approvals or pending webhook callbacks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Act on High Pending Counts" icon="triangle-exclamation" href="/user-guide/payments/transactions">
    A spike in Pending transactions often means the SMS parser device is offline or a gateway webhook is failing. Investigate immediately to avoid customer complaints about unconfirmed payments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lock Your Session" icon="lock" href="/user-guide/auth/login">
    Never leave the dashboard open on an unattended screen. Use the **User Menu** to log out, or ensure your OS screen lock activates quickly on idle.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use Global View for Reports" icon="chart-pie" href="/user-guide/dashboard/dashboard">
    When preparing cross-brand performance summaries, always switch to Global View first. Manually summing individual brand metrics introduces rounding and timing errors.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Likely Cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| All KPI cards show `0.00` and no transactions appear | The selected brand has no transactions for the chosen time range, or you are on a brand with zero history. | Switch the Time Range to **All Time** and verify the correct brand is selected. |
| "Global View" option is missing from the brand dropdown | Your account is a staff-level account without super-admin privileges. | Log in with the super-administrator account to access the global aggregation view. |
| Recent transactions list does not update after a new payment | The page may be cached. | Perform a hard refresh (`Ctrl+Shift+R` / `Cmd+Shift+R`) or navigate away and back to the dashboard. |
